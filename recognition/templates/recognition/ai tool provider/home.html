Our Project

Smart attendance System

The AI model first processes the image or video data to detect and track individual faces, using techniques like face detection, face recognition, and facial landmark detection. It can then use this information to identify the person and match it against a database of authorized individuals.

The system can also detect and differentiate between multiple people at the same time and keep track of the attendance records in real-time. This eliminates the need for manual attendance-taking and can save time and resources for educational institutions or organizations.
